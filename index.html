What are Conics?

Conic Sections

A conic section is a curve formed when a plane cuts a cone. The main conics are the circle, parabola, ellipse, and hyperbola.

Big idea

Each conic has its own shape, equation, and important parts. In this guide, we focus on recognizing the shape and reading its equation from a graph.
